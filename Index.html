The Farm That Made Me Rethink Travel

I had always thought of France as wine, cities, and super chic experiences until I spent a weekend in a farm in Provence.

🌱 Trigger: I needed a break from the big city and found this stay on a local eco-retreat platform.
🏞 Conflict: The initial days were challenging—early mornings, unfamiliar tasks, and a digital detox that left me uneasy.
🔄 First Plot Point: As I engaged in harvesting olives and tending to the garden, I began to appreciate the rhythms of farm life.
🛠 Second Plot Point: Sharing meals prepared from our harvest fostered a sense of community and purpose.
😓 Anticlimax: A sudden mistral wind damaged part of the garden, dampening spirits.
🌌 Dark Moment: I questioned my ability to adapt and considered shortening my stay.
💡 Climax: That night, we lit candles, shared stories, and I felt more connected than ever.
🌅 Finale: Departing the farm, I carried with me not just memories but a renewed appreciation for sustainable living and the profound connections forged through shared purpose.
